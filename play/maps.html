🖼️ Painted Area Maps

← all previews
Pre-rendered, hand-painted-style area backdrops in the lineage of the Infinity Engine — Baldur's Gate, Icewind Dale, Planescape: Torment. Moody, atmospheric, 2.5-D. Every pixel is original and procedural (no AI art, no scraped tiles): layered light, soft shadow, mottled texture, fog, torch-bloom and a heavy vignette, painted in code.
Regenerate with python3 tools/make-painted-maps.py (Pillow only). These are the painted backdrops a Unity build would composite walkable characters and props over — the same areas you can already walk in the 16-zone playable saga.
