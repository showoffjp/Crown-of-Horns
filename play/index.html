v6.81.0 · WHAT THE ROAD THROWS, MORE OF IT — a change of format after eight big multi-soul zones, back to the Wayward Mile caprices: short idiosyncratic random events that come out of nowhere, a handful per playthrough, now 16 in the pool (drawMax 4, so still rare), and all auto-crossing to Unity via the bridged draw router. THE DOOR (surreal/tender): a door standing alone in the road, no wall, no house, and something knocks from the inside — a soul who never left home and clings to a door that never enclosed anything; open it (there was never an in), KNOCK BACK (nobody ever knocks back), or sit in the doorway and bring the world to them (the out was never the thing they feared, it was being in it alone). THE UNDERSTUDY (theatrical/melancholy): a soul rehearsing to an empty grey house the one scene that matters — their own death, rewritten a hundred ways (brave, peaceful, and the true one: small, afraid, alone); pick which they keep, or refuse the exercise (a death isn't an ending, it's the shortest scene and the one you had least say in — you were a whole play). THE ARGUMENT (comedy to love story): two souls who've argued a century about whether a bird was a wren or a sparrow and won't dissolve until it's settled — settle it (Nature crit/fumble) and the anchor lifts and they drift apart content; be magnificently wrong (a chaffinch?!) and unite them against a common enemy for another happy century; or name what it actually is, a rope, the last thing that's theirs, and they hold it on purpose forever. Gate 801/0; full suite green. 84 zones, 265 souls, 28 quests, 16 caprices, 24 banters.
