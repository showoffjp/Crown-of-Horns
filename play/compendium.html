The shape of a playthrough — acts, eras, and the flags that gate them.

Act I — The Gate

Sword Coast

You wake Returned in Baldur's Gate. Hear the Herald, take the Cinderhaunt, and clear the prologue — the Wall's first whisper. Recruit Sister Garrow, Roen, Varra.

unlocks via prologue.cleared

Act II — The Fugue

The Fugue

Descend to the Wall of the Faithless. Maerin waits, half-unmade. Pulling her free costs a companion, forever — the Breach, the bill love runs up.

unlocks via (the permanent loss)

Act III — Netheril

Netheril

The first rift: a flying empire's last hour. Naeve, the falling city, the Mythallar Colossus. Clears → the Crown Wars open.

unlocks via netheril.cleared

Act III — Crown Wars

Crown Wars

The ancient court whose verdict first fed the Wall. Ilfaeril, the First Unmade, and the Verdict you can argue DOWN.

unlocks via crownwars.cleared / verdict_spared

Act IV — Time of Troubles

Time of Troubles

The year a god dies and his skull is beaten into the Crown of Horns — the relic Aldric carries. The Avatar of Bone.

unlocks via act4.toot_done

Act IV — Spellplague

Spellplague

Causality-optional blue fire, the Unmade closing in. The Herald of the Unmade. The last era before the Court.

unlocks via act4.spellplague_done

Finale — The Court of the Dead

The Court

Six endings, gated by what you understood: Abolition, Doomguide's Peace, the Returned Throne, Mortal Measure — and two GOLDEN roads, Jergal's Keyhole and Break the Loop.

unlocks via netheril.cleared → the Court
