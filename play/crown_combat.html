⚔️ Tactics

This skirmish runs BG3-class tactical rules. Here's the whole toolbox.

🔥 Environmental surfaces

  • Oil Flask lays grease → Firebrand ignites it; fire spreads tile-to-tile and burns whoever stands in it.
  • Hallowed Water lays water (and douses fire → steam, which gives +2 cover). Storm Bolt arcs through water, zapping everyone wet.
  • Venom Vial → a poison cloud; Frost → ice that makes foes slip (Slowed).

🗺️ Position

  • High ground (the gilded ▲ tiles) grants advantage on your attacks; striking from below gives disadvantage. The forecast already accounts for it.
  • Leaving an adjacent foe's reach provokes an opportunity attack — spend Disengage to move safely.
  • Shove a foe into your fire (or into a wall for impact damage).
  • Jump (range 3) vaults over walls and units to a clear tile — and provokes no opportunity attack.

🧭 Comfort

  • Undo Move takes back a clean move · Non-lethal spares the defeated · Reactions toggles your opportunity attacks.
  • Every target shows a live hit% · damage · kill% forecast; your selected hero shows incoming threat.
