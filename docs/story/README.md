### The shape of it, in three sentences
You die in the first hour and are dragged back — *Returned* — by a grieving cosmic absence
called **the Unmade**, the accreted nothing of every soul the gods ever discarded into the
**Wall of the Faithless**. To stop it (or join it) you must walk backward through the
catastrophes of Realms history, pursued by a hunter who wears your own face. And in the last
hour you learn the worst thing of all: the absence has no beginning, because **the first soul
it ever pulled back, ten thousand years ago, was you.**

*Lose sleep responsibly.*
